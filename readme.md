# LeagueTrendz [(Website)](https://leaguetrendz.web.app/)
### *A web app for NBA fans — Explore stats, team performances, and player data from 2024 and past seasons.*<br>

## Tech Stack PERN
- React with Typescript Frontend
- Express & Node Backend
- Supabase PostgreSQL Database

## Data Source
The data used in this project is sourced from [Basketball Reference](https://www.basketball-reference.com/), which provides comprehensive statistics and historical data on basketball players, teams, and games.

### Home

- **Championship Recap** – Features the NBA Finals matchup, series outcome, and Finals MVP.  
- **Popular Players** – Showcases familiar names from across the league.  
- **Season Highlights** – Showcases award winners, including MVP and other top accolades.  
- **Conference Standings** – Displays the regular season rankings for both the Eastern and Western Conferences.

### Schedule

- **Weekly Schedule** – Features all games scheduled during the week of the selected date.  
- **Game MVPs** – Highlights top players and their stats for each team and each game.  
- **Quick Links** – Links to important dates in the 2024 season - Season Start, Start of Playoffs, and Final Game of the Season.

### Standings

- **Sortable Standings Table** – View and sort team stats like wins, losses, games behind, points per game (PPG), and opponent PPG.  
- **Flexible Rankings** – Toggle between division, conference, and league-wide standings for a comprehensive view.  
- **Advanced Team Insights** – Explore detailed performance metrics, including each team's record against each division, conference, in close games, and blowouts.

### Game Log/Box Score

- **Full Team Box Score** – View detailed team stats, including field goals, three-pointers, free throws, rebounds, assists, steals, and more.  
- **Game Leaders** – Highlights the top performers from each team in points, rebounds, and assists.  
- **Head-to-Head Series Results** – Displays the season and playoff (if applicable) series outcomes between the two teams.  
- **Player Box Score** – Provides individual stats for every player who played in the game.

### Single Team

- **Team Overview & Rankings** – See recent games, season stats, conference standings, and team leaders on offense and defense.  
- **Player & Team Stats** – View individual player stats for the season and team leaders in key categories.  
- **Full Schedule & Roster** – Access a complete game log of the season and a full list of current players.  
- **Franchise History** – Explore championship wins, retired numbers, and a complete season-by-season breakdown, including past team names and outcomes.

### Single Player

- **Player Profile & Rankings** – Displays key bio details, draft info, and how the player's stats rank across the league.  
- **Season & Career Stats** – View detailed season-by-season stats with options to toggle between averages, totals, and regular season/playoff performances.  
- **Performance Insights** – Explore splits comparing home vs. away games, conference/division matchups, and performance in wins vs. losses.  
- **Game-by-Game Breakdown** – Track individual game stats for the entire season, including recent performances and last-game highlights.

### Season Leaders

- **Top Performers** – Displays the top five leaders in key stat categories like points, rebounds, and assists, with links to view the top 50.  
- **Detailed Leaderboards** – Provides an in-depth table of statistical leaders across multiple categories.  
- **Customizable Views** – Toggle between regular season and playoff stats, as well as per-game averages or total stats.

### Game Highs

- **Top Game Performances** – Highlights the top 5 players with the highest values in key stats like points, rebounds, and assists throughout the season, with links to the specific games.  
- **Record-Breaking Moments** – Track the most impressive individual game achievements in points, rebounds, assists, and more.  
- **Season vs Playoffs** – Toggle between regular season and playoff game highs to compare standout performances.
